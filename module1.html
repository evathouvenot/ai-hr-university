Prompt Engineering for HR

Master the CLEAR framework and write effective prompts for HR tasks

25 minutes

The CLEAR Framework for HR Prompts

C

Context

Who you are, what company, what role

L

Length

How long should the output be

E

Examples

Provide samples of what you want

A

Audience

Who will read/use this

R

Requirements

Specific constraints or must-haves

Prompt Transformation Example

‚ùå Bad Prompt

"Write a job description for a marketing manager"

‚úÖ Good Prompt

"I'm an HR Director at a 150-person SaaS company. Write a job description for a Senior Marketing Manager role. The description should be 300-400 words, appeal to candidates with 5-7 years experience, and emphasize our remote-first culture and growth opportunities. Include specific requirements for digital marketing, team leadership, and B2B experience. Use an engaging, conversational tone that reflects our innovative company culture."

Change Management for AI Adoption

Overcoming resistance and building team buy-in

15 minutes

Common Objections & Responses

"AI will replace HR jobs"
AI enhances human capabilities, allowing HR to focus on strategic, relationship-building activities that require emotional intelligence and complex decision-making.
"Our data isn't good enough"
AI can help improve data quality over time. Start with simple use cases and gradually expand as data improves.
"It's too expensive"
Many AI tools cost less than $20/month per user. The ROI from time savings typically pays for itself within 2-3 months.
"We don't have technical expertise"
Modern AI tools are designed for non-technical users. This course provides all the training needed to get started.
